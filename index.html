Nailin Mental Health With SKy

I'm Skyler Nelson or simply just Sky. I am a veteran advocate that served in the Army. During my time as a chaplain assistant, I developed a passion for mental health awareness and suicide prevention. I served a year tour in Iraq and know the challenges many of our veterans face. I believe that if we take care of mental health that we eliminate issues such as substance abuse, domestic violence, financial hardship, homelessness, and suicide.

One of my favorite non profits is K9s for Veterans Abroad which is run by Richard and Verena Rice. They provide highly trained and skilled service dogs to veterans in need. These dogs help improve the quality of life for veterans battling mental illness such as PTSD as well as physical injuries.
